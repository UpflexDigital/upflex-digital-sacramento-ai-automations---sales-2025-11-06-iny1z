About Upflex Digital

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Upflex Digital emerged from a vision to democratize access to cutting-edge AI technology and digital marketing expertise. Our founders recognized that Sacramento's thriving business community deserved world-class solutions without the enterprise price tag. What started as a small team of passionate developers and digital strategists has grown into a trusted partner for hundreds of businesses across the region. We've helped startups scale rapidly and established companies modernize their operations through intelligent automation and data-driven marketing strategies.
